Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need any prior German knowledge to start?
No. We offer beginner-level (A1) classes designed for those with zero experience.

2. How do I book my free trial class?
Simply fill out the form at the top of the page. Choose your level, and you’ll receive a confirmation email with class details.

3. Which materials do I need?
All you need is a smartphone, a reliable internet connection, and a headset or headphones with a microphone.
